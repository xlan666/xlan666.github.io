LENS-Performance - 2025 ANU

Performance notes

This LENS performance was used for teaching and presented in COMP8350 Sound and Music Computing at the Australian National University, focusing on live collaborative computer music. I designed a computer-based music system for group performance and presented it through a 4-5 minute live work built around sound structure, interface control and performer coordination.

I designed three operating interfaces and treated them as shared performance surfaces. Visual cues, timed structures and sound-control modules helped performers coordinate rhythm while leaving room for live variation. The piece focuses on collaborative control, readable screen-based interaction and the translation of interface gestures into musical texture.
