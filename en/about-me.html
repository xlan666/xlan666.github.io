I'm Yijia Wang, a human-centred creative technologist with a Monash University background in information technology, interactive media and game development, and postgraduate training in Computing and Human-Centred Creative Computing at the Australian National University.

My work moves across HCI, extended reality, Unity development, game design and development, sound interaction, digital media and creative technology. I design interactive systems from concept and user flow through to playable prototypes, spatial interfaces and polished visual presentation.

I am interested in turning technology into expressive experience across interaction design, UX/HCI, creative coding, game systems and immersive digital products. Across my projects, I focus on clear interaction logic, atmospheric worldbuilding and interfaces that feel natural to explore.
