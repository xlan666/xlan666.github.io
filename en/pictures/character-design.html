Character Design

Project notes

These works present 3D character models and animation-oriented character showcases that I designed and recreated. The collection focuses on character detail, proportion, expression, rhythm and balance while considering readability and expressive presence in dynamic game environments. Through modelling, material work, pose design and final rendering, I developed characters from concept into polished visual assets, reflecting an integrated understanding of character form, animation logic and visual storytelling.
