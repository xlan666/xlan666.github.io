Augmented Reality / Virtual Reality

Project notes

This augmented reality / virtual reality collection explores immersive applications, spatial interaction and virtual scene design. I used Unity, 3D modelling, rendering, lighting, sound effects, particle systems and interaction design to build virtual environments that can be explored through teleportation. These works focus on experiential logic and spatial storytelling: interactive objects, hidden areas, environmental cues and atmospheric feedback guide users as they read and understand each scene, bringing together technical implementation, visual expression and user experience.
