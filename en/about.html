About

Designing interactive experiences across people, systems and creative media

I am Yijia Wang, a human-centred creative technologist with an undergraduate background in IT and Interactive Media, and postgraduate training in Computing and Human-Centred Creative Computing.

My practice spans HCI, extended reality, Unity development, game development, sound interaction, digital media and creative technology. I work from research and interaction strategy into working prototypes, playable systems, spatial interfaces and visual production.

I am interested in turning technology into expressive digital experience across extended reality, Unity development, interactive installations, game systems, UX/HCI products and creative technology platforms. My projects centre on readable interaction, strong atmosphere and systems that invite people to explore through movement, play and feedback.
